getCrowder, it is an engine that allow you link multiple devices (controllers) like mobile, tablets and other computers, to a single computer (host) Through these devices you can perform actions that run on the host. Taking our sample application, the host is the board on which players play answers questions through their devices

How it works?

  • Multidevice connection.

  • Use your own device as a controller.

  • Multiplayer allowed.

  • Mutibrowser.

  • Warning! Extremely addictive.
